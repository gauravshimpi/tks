About Us

We Help Our Clients To Grow Their Business

Founded in 2018 following a decade of conceptualization, TKS Business Solutions stands as a financial consulting firm, providing an array of services such as project report preparation, financial feasibility studies, company valuation, MIS, costing, and management consultancy, Tax Filing Services (Clients from India & Abroad), Accounting outsourcing (Clients from India & Abroad).
